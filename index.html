About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the University of Sydney, supervised by Prof. Tongliang Liu. I received my Master of Computer Science from the University of Sydney in 2025. Before turning to academia, I worked as a Product Manager in Ads at Tencent. My research focuses on Generative AI Safety and Intellectual Property (IP) Protection for AI models, motivated by the need to build generative AI systems that are not only powerful, but also safe and trustworthy.
